## 🌟 What is CasaClean?

**CasaClean** is a full-stack web app where customers can browse cleaning services, pick a date, and book a professional cleaner for their home — quick, simple, and stress-free. 🧼🛋️

No more phone calls. No more waiting. Just a few clicks and your home gets the love it deserves. 💫

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔐 **Secure accounts** — sign up & log in with encrypted passwords and JWT auth
- 🧽 **Browse services** — explore different cleaning options and pricing
- 📅 **Easy booking** — choose a service, date & time in seconds
- 🏙️ **City coverage** — find available cleaners in your area
- 👤 **User profiles** — manage your bookings in one place
- 🍪 **Smart sessions** — stay logged in safely with secure cookies

---

## 🛠️ Built With

- ⚙️ **Node.js + Express** — fast & reliable backend
- 🍃 **MongoDB + Mongoose** — flexible data storage
- 🔑 **JWT + bcrypt** — rock-solid authentication
- 🌐 **CORS + Cookies** — smooth & secure client communication
